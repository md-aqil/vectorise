Before anything else, we are a community for creators – a space where ordinary content makers can be better advocates. The platform is for influencers who are trying to make it big or have made already made it.
We know that everyone is an influencer in their niche. We know that every voice matters and we want those voices heard. We want to democratise influence. Our chief purpose is to let you, the user, focus on creating your content, while we take the onus of finding brands that use that content and pay you for it!
It accelerates your career through collaboration opportunities with top brands and gives you access to a community full of experts in the influencer space.
We’ve made joining laughably simple. Here’s what you’ve gotta do…
  1. Sign up like you would for every other app.
  2. Verify yourself by clicking on the link we send to your email.
  3. Link the DYT account to your social media profile. You can link to Facebook, Twitter or Instagram right now. (all of them, two of them or just one of them – the choice is yours)
  4. Fill in your bio and done!
  1. Open the DYT app.
  2. Choose and click on the campaign you want the world to know about.
  3. Scroll down to find the ‘create content’ button and tap it.
  4. Select the photo you want to upload. You can pick one from your gallery, click one at the spot or if you can’t find the perfect fit choose one of our templates!
  5. Apply a filter (yes, just like you would on other apps) and tap next.
  6. Write your caption or choose one of ours.
  7. Send your post for approval (if the campaign requires it).
  8. Once approved, you can post it on Facebook, Twitter or Instagram by clicking the ‘share post’ button.
The amount you get paid depends upon your exact numerical DYT score, which is calculated and updated every day based on several metrics like interaction and connection with your audience. What’s great is that the score can be increased, which means you can earn more with time. Swing by our scoring policy to know more about it!s
We’ve made it very simple. You create content, post it on your profile, and the DYT app on your phone gets credit. These credits can be redeemed and sent as money to your bank or Paytm account.
1 credit is worth 1 INR.
  1. Open the DYT app.
  2. Tap on the ‘Wallet’ tab. (That’s the third tab on the bottom of your screen).
  3. Scroll to the ‘Redeem Credits’ button.
  4. Enter the amount of credits you want to redeem.
  5. Choose either your PayTM or Bank Account.
  6. Click on ‘Redeem Now’ button and you're done!
  7. We’ll even send you a success mail soon.
